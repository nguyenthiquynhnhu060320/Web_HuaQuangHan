Greg Hsu, born on October 31, 1990, is a prominent Taiwanese actor and singer. He gained widespread recognition for his role in the 2019 drama "Someday or One Day" and has since showcased his versatility in various TV dramas and films. Hsu made his film debut with the critically acclaimed "A Sun" in 2019. He continues to impress with his acting and musical talents, as seen in his recent film "18x2 Beyond Youthful Days" released in April 2024. Known for his charming personality and dedication to his craft, Hsu's slogan is "May this world remain as beautiful as it was in the beginning.
